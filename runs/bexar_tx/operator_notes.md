## general

- **2026-05-14T15:55:00Z (recon observation):** Bexar County (San Antonio metro) is the 4th-most-populous Texas county. State-mandated non-judicial foreclosure — trustee sales the first Tuesday of each month at the west side of the Bexar County Courthouse (between Courthouse and Paul Elizondo Tower); first-Wednesday fallback when January 1 or July 4 falls on a Tuesday. The County Clerk records ALL trustee sale notices in the recorded-instrument index AND publishes them on a public foreclosure map at `https://maps.bexar.org/foreclosures` with CSV export — this map is the easiest path to daily-fresh distress leads and should be the MVP source.

## clerk_recordings

## foreclosure_notices_map

- **2026-05-14T15:55:00Z (recon observation):** `https://maps.bexar.org/foreclosures` is a Bexar County Clerk-published live map of the *current month's* mortgage AND tax foreclosure notices. Free, no login. Exposes a data table view, CSV export, and a downloadable PDF of the full list. Links out to document PDFs. This is the strongest single P0 source for daily-fresh distress in Bexar — it's both authoritative (County Clerk publishes it) and operationally easy (CSV export removes any portal-scraping headache). Recommend MVP build off this source first.

- **2026-05-14T16:53:00Z (Phase 2 build):** ArcGIS REST endpoint located at `https://maps.bexar.org/arcgis/rest/services/CC/ForeclosuresProd/MapServer` (CC folder = County Clerk). Two layers: `0/Mortgage` and `1/Tax`. Both layers share the same 10-field schema: `OBJECTID, ADDRESS, DOC_NUMBER, YEAR, MONTH, SCHOOL_DIST, TYPE, CITY, ZIP, Shape`. `maxRecordCount: 1000`. Pagination via `resultOffset`. First live pull (2026-05-14T16:53:00Z) returned **288 records** (273 mortgage + 15 tax). Distribution: 163 records dated June 2026, 111 dated May 2026, 12 from April 2026, 1 from Feb 2026, 1 from Dec 2025 (these last carry-overs are likely postponed sales or notices recorded earlier that remain active). All records carry DOC_NUMBER and ADDRESS — parser_confidence 95 across the board. The `TYPE` field is coarse (just "MORTGAGE" or "TAX") — the specific instrument doc-type (Notice of Substitute Trustee's Sale, Notice of Trustee's Sale, etc.) lives in the actual recorded document at `bexar.tx.publicsearch.us`, accessible by joining on DOC_NUMBER. Geocode geometry uses NAD83 / Texas South Central (FIPS 4204) — wkid 102739 / 2278 — points are in feet. Top-10 cities: San Antonio (253), Converse (15), Boerne (3), then 1-2 each across Adkins, Helotes, Saint Hedwig, Universal City, Von Ormy, Live Oak, Somerset.
